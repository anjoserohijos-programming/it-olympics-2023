Here are some of the best outputs created by the students of FEU Alabang!

Arc Star - Bewildered Being

Bewildered Being is an Action-Adventure Role-Playing Game for PC that focuses on various European Mythological Creatures. The Homunculus, a construct that acquired sentience as a result of a laboratory accident, will be under the player's control. In order to learn more about who built the Homunculus, players will be interacting with the people, fighting creatures, and exploring Erdwell. The game features different stages that the player can explore, various monsters based on different European Mythologies, and a Karma System where the player can choose what form to take.

FinkaBoost - Trials for Reincarnation

"Trials for Reincarnation" is an action-packed top-down 3D shooter game where players take on the role of Apokali, an ordinary man who was killed and reincarnated as an experimental defect of the Terrans. But with this new form comes great power, and players must use it to battle their way through hordes of Terrans in order to be reincarnated again and return to the human world. As Apokali, players will fight their way through treacherous levels, each one more challenging than the last. You'll have to be quick on your feet and strategic with your abilities as you take on hordes of enemies, from common grunts to powerful bosses. With immersive gameplay and a thrilling story, "Trials for Reincarnation'' is guaranteed to give players a pulse-pounding experience as they fight for their chance at reincarnation and return to the human world.

Nouvelaro - Marahuyo

Marahuyo will take on the genre of a Real-Time Strategy game that revolves around the Spanish Inquisition in the setting of the Philippine Islands in which the game begins on Magellan’s arrival. The main context of the game will cover the historical events that happened earlier in our time with a small mix of humor to add a twist to the gameplay and entertainment for the players. A Real-Time Strategy (RTS) game is a subgenre of strategy games which allows players to simulate battles by controlling the units without waiting for turns.

PolaRis - Himekari

Himekari is an adventure-themed mobile game with rhythmic beat ‘em up mechanics. The mechanics of the game is a hybrid mix of rhythm and beat ‘em up meaning players are tasked to use their cognitive skills to finish beat maps as they beat enemies called the Refrains. The game shares a story of a group called the Guardians of Light and a mercenary named Rune, who gets pulled into their quest of capturing the thieves and stop the awakening of Tree Song. Join them as Rune starts to realize that these girls are more than what they seem to be. A harmonious journey and adventure to protect the World Score.

Quad Squad - Mirror Madness

Quad Squad Mirror Madness A Sci-Fi, PVP, First Person Shooter-Game game designed for the PC platform, a game where the players can have a fun and challenging experience with their friends and fellow online gamers.

Whelmed - eSakai

eSakai is a single-player, 3D adventure dating simulator available to play on the PC. While driving mythological creatures around locations inspired by FEU Alabang's surrounding area, they also cultivate relationships with personalities they meet and maybe find romance down the road.

AGLT - Yugto

Due to the lack of history learning in the Philippines from the way of teaching (1) and error abundance in textbooks (2), the proponents, AGLT, came up with an interactive solution to supplement textbook knowledge on Filipino historical figures during the Spanish era. YUGTO offers interactivity in the form of a game via: visual assets (2D & 3D), RPG mechanics, and; narratives. With the help of these elements, the project aims to use game-based learning in making history learning appealing to learners. True to its Tagalog meaning "phase" or "stage," YUGTO hopes to bring Filipinos' enthusiasm in PH history and culture to the next level.

GraficO - kARTribo

kARTribo is an interactive 3D motion art gallery that exhibits the rich artistry and culture of the Southern Tagalog regions in the Philippines, namely CALABARZON and MIMAROPA regions. kARTribo’s etymology is inspired by indigenous tribe members who are often called “ka-tribo” in Filipino. This project is both for web and virtual reality platforms where viewers, or the travelers, could enjoy and interact with the virtual gallery via their personal computer devices, or via their Android mobile devices that are compatible with a Virtual-Reality headset. One can easily find both traditional and digital works of art from the Southern Tagalog regions on this project, which brands itself as "Your Pinoy Art Destination." Additionally, travelers can immerse themselves in the rich motion graphics of most art pieces and can expect other gallery features that allows them to reach the artist behind a masterpiece. The immersive art viewing experience offered by kARTribo strives to bridge the gap between local artists and art enthusiasts that can transcend geographical limits.

Tech Ascenders - The Saga of The Morn Maleficum

The Saga of the Morn Maleficum, A Tale of Science and Magic is a graphic novel for the PC. It contains elements of both science fiction and fantasy. Science fantasy is a type of speculative fiction that features future technologies and magic. Built as an interactive visual novel that emphasizes the usage of 3D images in the construction of VNs. Some of the mechanics of the system are similar to those found in a standard visual novel. Dialogue boxes emerge on occasion in frames that allude to the show's association with the visual novel. Various camera angles were used to enhance the mood and impact the viewer's sense of different levels of intimacy and distance. Every scene in the story is built in 3D with a few animations. There is also a look-around option, although the camera will lock at a specific angle. At some point in the story, cutscenes will appear to highlight/amplify an important event or circumstance.

Analytico - Asfalis

Asfalis originates from the greek word "ΑΣΦΑΛΙΣΗ" or "Asfalisi" which means insurance or security. This project involves the design and development of a web-based system that aims to improve and optimize customer retention strategies of car insurance companies using Cohort and Churn analysis. The system employs descriptive analytics using historical customer cohort data and predictive analytics using a machine learning algorithm for churn prediction. With the information gathered from the data analysis tools, the system generates meaningful insights and interpretations that can provide the car insurance company with optimized customer retention strategies.

BlockChains - ProReco

ProReco is a name that came from the words 'Procurement' and 'Recommendation'. This project aims to optimize the procurement strategy through designing and developing a web-based recommendation system through using decision-tree and key performance indicator (KPI) analysis. From getting the inventory and sales data the system will then use the data analysis tool that will help analyze the status of the supply and demand rate for the system to generate a recommendation that needs immediate attention that will help the business sustain the products and improve its revenue growth perspective.

CapsLock - We Care

We Care is a web-based customer feedback system that collects data to comprehend customers' insights and concerns and employs Sentiment Analysis to optimize customer feedback for Kumpares PH. With the aid of a sentiment analysis algorithm, the customer feedback will be evaluated, filtered, and categorized into positive, neutral, and negative feedback. We Care is essential for giving the management important information to guide their decision-making and influence creative modifications to their current product or service. This will gauge customer satisfaction as well as help Kumpares PH retain more customers.

Codesque - INVION

INVION is a tool for businesses to use to optimize their inventory through various data analysis techniques as well as data management. Data Analysis and Data Management are two components that are essential for businesses to thrive and INVION not only offers one but both of these vital business elements. INVION has a variety of features from User Account Management, Audit Logs, Data Analysis, Point of Sale and more. INVION will be the tomorrow that will shape businesses for the better

Highminds - Paperas

The goal of Paperas is to create a system that would be able to help our clients find and study their revenue indicators in order to help improve their business. The clients will be using Market Basket Analysis to determine their revenue indicators based on their customers purchasing patterns through their transactional data. With the information gathered, they will be able to use our system that utilizes the FP Growth algorithm in order to help them visualize the data they have gathered in order for them to be able to create marketing strategies in order to optimize their revenue. It is critical for a small business to understand its revenue indicators in order to accurately monitor their business revenue.

Infinity Capstones - MyThrift

MyThrift is a web-based inventory management system that uses ABC Analysis to optimize inventory. This project focuses on setting up an online e-commerce platform with an inventory management system and ordering system, along with the ability to showcase products and how these products perform. With the implementation of ABC Analysis, the system determines slow moving and off-season products in the inventory, providing insight in order to generate reports based on its sales. Leveraging the information collected, the system produces clear and concise evaluations and analyses that help produce data-driven decision making.

Keystrokes - Mercantile

“Mercantile” (pronounced “Mer-Kan-Till”) derives from the French term of the same lexicon pertaining to merchants, trade, or commerce which in the project’s context focuses on the business and client of a grocery store. This capstone project centers around creating a web-based dashboard for inventory management and sales optimization using ABC analysis for the R. Cernio store, a Micro-retail grocery. The system aims to have features native to similar systems, such as report generation, inventory features, Point-of-sale-esqe characteristics, stock notification, and marketing techniques. The project aims to help benefit the owners of the R. Cernio store by taking note of stock quantity and creating analytical classifications of product value based on the ABC results. Through this, getting rid of unwanted occurrences such as stockout and unawareness of product demand are issues the system aims to tackle.

SAD4 - Kha - hoon

Kha-hoon is an online inventory management and sales system developed for Miyerkules Boutique. It records and manages inventory data, handles transactions between the client and customers, and forecasts sales using mean reversion and time-series analysis. The system requires users to create an account for security and database management purposes. Data collected from transactions is used to forecast best-selling items and estimate revenue during dry and rainy seasons, enabling the client to manage their inventory and restock as needed.

Thinking Tree - Bin Yang

Bin Yang is a web-based system with forecasting allowing the company to utilize a rule engine to execute the company’s operational decision. It is named after the client’s company Bin Yang, a coffee and tea shop. It has various features from the customer side such as cart system for online ordering, payment system that offers 2 modes of payment, profile for both customer and admin, menu page, contact page and for the admin side it has dashboard, inventory system for resource monitoring, user management system to monitor users for both customer and admin, order management to monitor orders, and lastly sales report and sales forecast where in the website reports the current sales status and the forecasting is being applied by using data that is provided by the client to forecast its future sales that is showed in sales forecast.

To Follow Up - MotorSmoothy

A web-based motor parts recommendation system utilizing ABC Analysis for Lucky M2M Motoshop is a web-based inventory system that can benefit motorcycle shops. Through this system, the business owner can monitor their inventory. This system will determine which products can be paired using ABC analysis and Market Basket Analysis, which results in the recommendation system. Big Deepfreeze - VetCare

This capstone project focuses on developing a dual-platform appointment system “VetCare” that would benefit the appointment scheduling and management of South AH Vet Inc. in Alabang. VetCare is an appointment system that shows the appointments for the day and all the corresponding details, developing a web and mobile application that showcases the clinic and allows customers to book appointments conveniently, and providing a way to store customers’ pet records and allow clerks to create new records, read and update existing records.

Rosas

ROSAS is a special ecommerce platform developed to cater the transaction between a floral vendor and their customers. Just like any other ecommerce platforms, the customers can browse, add items to cart, buy, and monitor the status of the delivery. In addition, the customers may also edit the bouquet designs provided by the seller. This capstone project also has an administration website, where the seller can add new products, and create new bouquet designs for their customers. It also allows the seller to manage the orders, prepare, and ship them to the preferred location of the customers. The administration website also includes inventory analysis and sales revenue monitoring to help the seller to keep track and control the significant products as well as assisting the seller to make clear decisions from the information it provides.

T - Spin - R&R

Emergency response time is very important. It is affected by many local factors, such as a growing population, traffic congestion, and other transportation issues. During emergency situations, sometimes the rescuers find it hard to locate the person in need of rescue. Further delay in emergency response will increase the risk to the lives of many. Thus, we propose R&R: Rescue and Respond, a web and mobile application for self-emergency pinging and locating systems with the integration of Assisted Global Positioning System (A-GPS). In this proposed study, Geolocation and Google Maps API is to be utilized. We will be using the agile method, namely the scrum framework, for the development process of this application.
